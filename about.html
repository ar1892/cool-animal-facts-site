About This Site

Hi! I'm Andres Raygada, and I created this website as part of a class project to explore the unique and incredible biodiversity of Peru.

As someone who loves both technology and nature, I wanted to showcase some of the amazing animals that call Peru home. This site highlights both well-known and rare creatures from the jungle, mountains, rivers, and coasts of Peru.

My goal is to raise awareness and appreciation for Peruvian wildlife, while building a fully responsive, multi-page website using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. Everything you see here is powered by GitHub Pages.
